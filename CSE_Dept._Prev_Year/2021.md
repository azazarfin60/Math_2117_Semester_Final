### SECTION - A

**1(a)** Find the conditions of three vector are linear dependent and independent. **(04)**

**1(b)** Find the projection of the vector

$$
\vec{A} = \vec{i} - 2\vec{j} + \vec{k}
$$

on the vector

$$
\vec{B} = 4\vec{i} - 4\vec{j} + 7\vec{k}
$$

. **(03)**

**1(c)** Find the work done in moving an object along a straight line from $(3, 2, -1)$ to $(2, -1, 4)$ in a force field given by

$$
\vec{F} = 4\vec{i} - 3\vec{j} + 2\vec{k}
$$

. **(03)**

**2(a)** State and prove Frenet-Serret Formulae. **(05)**

**2(b)** Prove that: **(02)**

$$
\vec{A} \cdot \frac{d\vec{A}}{dt} = A \frac{dA}{dt}
$$

**2(c)** A particle moves along the curve $x = 2t^2$, $y = t^2 - 4t$, $z = 3t - 5$, where $t$ is the time. Find the components of its velocity and acceleration at time $t = 1$ in the direction: **(03)**

$$
\vec{i} - 3\vec{j} + 2\vec{k}
$$

**3(a)** Show that $\vec{\nabla}\phi$ is a vector perpendicular to the surface $\phi(x,y,z) = c$ where $c$ is a constant. **(02)**

**3(b)** Find the directional derivative of $P = 4e^{2x-y+z}$ at the point $(1, 1, -1)$ in the direction toward the point $(-3, 5, 6)$. **(03)**

**3(c)** Evaluate: **(02)**

$$
\int_2^3 \vec{A} \cdot \frac{d\vec{A}}{dt} dt
$$

if

$$
\vec{A}(2) = 2\vec{i} - \vec{j} + 2\vec{k} \quad \text{and} \quad \vec{A}(3) = 4\vec{i} - 2\vec{j} + 3\vec{k}
$$

.

**3(d)** If

$$
\vec{F} = (5xy - 6x^2)\vec{i} + (2y - 4x)\vec{j}
$$

, evaluate: **(03)**

$$
\int_C \vec{F} \cdot d\vec{r}
$$

along the curve $C$ in the $xy$ plane, $y = x^3$ from the point $(1, 1)$ to $(2, 8)$.

**4(a)** State and prove Stokes theorem. **(05)**

**4(b)** Verify divergence theorem for

$$
\vec{A} = 2x^2y\vec{i} - y^2\vec{j} + 4xz^2\vec{k}
$$

taken over the region in the first octant bounded by $y^2 + z^2 = 9$ and $x = 2$. **(05)**

### SECTION - B

**5(a)** Define symmetric and skew-Hermitian matrices with example. **(02)**

**5(b)** Show that every square matrix can be uniquely expressed as the sum of a symmetric and skew-symmetric matrices. **(03)**

**5(c)** Define rank of a matrix. Reduce the matrix

$$
A = \begin{pmatrix}
1 & -1 & 2 & -3 \\
4 & 1 & 0 & 2 \\
0 & 3 & 0 & 4 \\
0 & 1 & 0 & 2
\end{pmatrix}
$$

into normal form and hence find its rank. **(05)**

**6(a)** Apply rank test to examine of the following system of equations:

$$
\begin{aligned}
x - 3y - 8z &= -10 \\
3x + y - 4z &= 0 \\
2x + 5y + 6z &= 13
\end{aligned}
$$

is consistent and if consistent find the complete solution. **(05)**

**6(b)** Find the eigen values and eigen vectors of the following matrix: **(05)**

$$
A = \begin{pmatrix}
1 & 0 & -1 \\
1 & 2 & 1 \\
2 & 2 & 3
\end{pmatrix}
$$

**7(a)** Find the dimension and a basis of the solution space $W$ of the system: **(05)**

$$
\begin{aligned}
x + 2y + 2z - s + 3t &= 0 \\
x + 2y + 3z + s + t &= 0 \\
3x + 6y + 8z + s + 5t &= 0
\end{aligned}
$$

**7(b)** Let $T$ be the linear operator on $\mathbb{R}^3$ defined by:

$$
T(x, y, z) = (2y + z, x - 4y, 3x)
$$

Find the matrix of $T$ in the basis $\lbrace f_1 = (1, 1, 1), f_2 = (1, 1, 0), f_3 = (1, 0, 0)\rbrace$. **(05)**

**8(a)** Let $W$ be the space generated by the polynomials:

$$
v_1 = t^3 - 2t^2 + 4t + 1, \quad v_2 = 2t^3 - 3t^2 + 9t - 1, \quad v_3 = t^3 + 6t - 5, \quad v_4 = 2t^3 - 5t^2 + 7t + 5
$$

Determine a basis and the dimension of $W$. **(05)**

**8(b)** Let $F: \mathbb{R}^4 \to \mathbb{R}^3$ be a linear mapping defined by:

$$
F(x, y, s, t) = (x - y + s + t, x + 2s - t, x + y + 3s - 3t)
$$

Find a basis and the dimension of the: **(05)**
*   (i) image $U$ of $F$
*   (ii) kernel $W$ of $F$
