### SECTION - A

**1(a)** Two sides of a triangle are formed by the vectors

$$
\vec{A} = 3\hat{i} + 6\hat{j} - 2\hat{k}
$$

and

$$
\vec{B} = 4\hat{i} - \hat{j} + 3\hat{k}
$$

Determine the angles of the triangle. **(03)**

**1(b)** Given the space curve: **(04)**

$$
x = a \cos t, \quad y = a \sin t, \quad z = bt
$$

Find the radius of curvature.

**1(c)** Find an equation for the tangent plane to the surface

$$
xz^2 + x^2y = z - 1
$$

at the point $(1, -3, 2)$. **(03)**

**2(a)** Evaluate: **(05)**

$$
\iint_S \phi \hat{n} dS
$$

if

$$
\phi = 4x + 3y - 2z
$$

and $S$ is the surface of $2x + y + 2z = 6$ bounded by $x=0$, $x=1$, $y=0$ and $y=2$.

**2(b)** Evaluate: **(05)**

$$
\iiint_V (2x + y) dV
$$

where $V$ is the closed region bounded by the cylinder $z = 4 - x^2$ and the planes $x = 0$, $y = 0$, $y = 2$ and $z = 0$.

**3(a)** State and prove divergence theorem. **(05)**

**3(b)** Verify Green's theorem for the plane: **(05)**

$$
\oint_C xy^2 dx + (y - 6) dy
$$

where $C$ is defined as the rectangle with vertices $(1, -1)$, $(4, -1)$, $(4, 2)$ and $(1, 2)$, and the rectangle is positively oriented.

**4(a)** State Green's theorem. Verify Green's theorem in the plane for: **(05)**

$$
\oint_C (xy + y^2) dx + x^2 dy
$$

where $C$ is the closed curve of the region bounded by $y = x$ and $y = x^2$.

**4(b)** State divergence theorem and verify for: **(05)**

$$
\vec{F} = 4xz\hat{i} - y^2\hat{j} + yz\hat{k}
$$

taken over the region bounded by $x = 0$, $x = 1$, $y = 0$, $y = 1$, $z = 0$, and $z = 1$.

### SECTION - B

**5(a)** Define the followings with example/properties:
(i) Product of two matrices, (ii) Hermitian matrix, (iii) Skew-symmetric matrix. **(03)**

**5(b)** Find the adjoint and inverse (if possible) of: **(04)**

$$
A = \begin{pmatrix}
1 & 4 & 3 \\
1 & 3 & 3 \\
1 & 2 & 5
\end{pmatrix}
$$

**5(c)** Define rank of a matrix and find the rank of: **(03)**

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
4 & 8 & 12 & 2 \\
1 & 2 & 3 & 3 \\
2 & 4 & 6 & -1
\end{pmatrix}
$$

**6(a)** Define vector space with example. Let $V$ be the vector space of $2 \times 2$ matrices over $\mathbb{R}$ and let:

$$
M = \begin{pmatrix}
1 & 2 \\
0 & 3
\end{pmatrix}
$$

Let $F: V \to V$ be the linear map defined by $F(A) = AM - MA$. Find a basis and the dimension of the kernel $W$ of $F$. **(05)**

**6(b)** Let $T$ be the linear operator on $\mathbb{R}^3$ defined by:

$$
T(x, y, z) = (2y + z, x - 4y, 3x)
$$

Find the matrix of $T$ in the basis $f = \lbrace(1, 1, 1), (1, 1, 0), (1, 0, 0)\rbrace$ and verify that: **(05)**

$$
[T]_f [v]_f = [T(v)]_f
$$

**7(a)** Define linear operator. Suppose $T: \mathbb{R}^3 \to \mathbb{R}^3$ is defined by: **(05)**

$$
T(x, y, z) = (x + 2y - z, z + y, x + y - 2z)
$$

Find a basis and dimension of the:
*   (i) image $U$ of $T$
*   (ii) kernel $W$ of $T$

**7(b)** Define Hilbert-space. Discuss the matrix representation of operator in Hilbert-space. **(05)**

**8(a)** Find the dimension and a basis of the solution space $W$ of the system: **(05)**

$$
\begin{aligned}
x + 2y + 2z - s + 3t &= 0 \\
x + 2y + 2z + s + t &= 0 \\
3x + 6y + 8z + s + 5t &= 0
\end{aligned}
$$

**8(b)** Let $U$ and $W$ be the following subspaces of $\mathbb{R}^4$: **(05)**

$$
U = \lbrace(a, b, c, d) \mid b + c + d = 0\rbrace
$$

$$
W = \lbrace(a, b, c, d) \mid a + b = 0, \quad c = 2d\rbrace
$$

Find the basis and the dimension of:
*   (i) $U$
*   (ii) $W$
*   (iii) $U \cap W$
*   (iv) $U + W$
