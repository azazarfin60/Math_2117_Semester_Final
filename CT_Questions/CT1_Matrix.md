# Class Test-1
**Course Code: MATH-2117**
**Full Marks: 20, Time: 45 minutes**

> N.B.: Answer all the questions

---

**1.** Consider a circuit with three mesh currents. The corresponding equations for the mesh currents $i_1$, $i_2$, and $i_3$ are: **(07)**

$$
2i_1 + 3i_2 - i_3 = 5
$$

$$
i_1 - 2i_2 + 4i_3 = 8
$$

$$
3i_1 + i_2 + 2i_3 = -3
$$

Solve this system of linear equations by reducing its augmented matrix to echelon form to find the mesh currents.

**2.** Find the value(s) of $k$ for which the following system of three linear equations in three variables has (i) a unique solution, (ii) no solution, and (iii) infinitely many solutions: **(06)**

$$
x + y + z = 3
$$

$$
2x + 3y + z = 7
$$

$$
3x + 5y + kz = 10
$$

**3.** What is meant by the rank of a matrix? Reduce the matrix $A = \begin{bmatrix} 6 & 3 & -4 \\ -4 & 1 & -6 \\ 1 & 2 & -5 \end{bmatrix}$ **(07)**

*   (i) to echelon form
*   (ii) to row canonical form, and
*   (iii) determine the rank of the matrix.
