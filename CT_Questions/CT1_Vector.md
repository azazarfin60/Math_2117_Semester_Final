# CT 1 / Math 2117 (ECE'23)
**Time: 20 min**
**Marks: 20**

---

**1.** Explain the physical significance of dot and cross product. Show that 

$$
\vec{A} = \frac{1}{3}(2\hat{i} - 2\hat{j} + \hat{k})
$$

, 

$$
\vec{B} = \frac{1}{3}(\hat{i} + 2\hat{j} + 2\hat{k})
$$

 and 

$$
\vec{C} = \frac{1}{3}(2\hat{i} + \hat{j} - 2\hat{k})
$$

 are mutually orthogonal unit vectors. **(2+2+6)**

**2.** State Frenet-Serret formulas. Show that $\vec{r} = e^{-t}(\vec{A} \cos 2t + \vec{B} \sin 2t)$, where $\vec{A}$ and $\vec{B}$ are constant vectors, is a solution of the differential equation $\frac{d^2\vec{r}}{dt^2} + 2\frac{d\vec{r}}{dt} + 5\vec{r} = 0$. **(2+8)**
