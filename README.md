## 📋 Course Syllabus at a Glance

**Vector Analysis** — Vectors, differentiation & integration, line/surface/volume integrals, gradient, divergence, curl, vector identities, Green's, Gauss', and Stokes' theorems.

**Matrix Theory** — Matrix operations, rank, normal form, systems of linear equations, eigenvalues & eigenvectors, Cayley-Hamilton theorem, matrix differential equations.

**Linear Algebra** — Vector spaces, subspaces, direct sums, basis & dimension, linear transformations, kernel & image, inner product spaces, normed linear spaces, Banach & Hilbert spaces.
