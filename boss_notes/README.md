### Section A — Vector Analysis (18 notes)

> *From direction cosines to the three grand theorems of vector calculus.*

> *From basic operations to diagonalization and differential equations.*

**Properties & Forms (B01 – B06):** Matrix types, transpose, Hermitian, proofs, echelon form, rank, canonical form

**Systems of Equations (B07 – B09):** Row reduction, parameter analysis (λ, μ), homogeneous systems

**Fundamentals (C01 – C03):** Field axioms, vector spaces, subspaces, direct sums

**Independence & Basis (C04 – C06):** Linear combinations, span, dependence/independence, basis, dimension

**Transformations & Spaces (C07 – C12):** Linear maps, kernel/image, change of basis, inner product spaces, Banach & Hilbert spaces
